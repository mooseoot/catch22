CATCH 22 ITEM TRACKER

Challenge Image:

Challenge Rules:

Collect exactly 22 Big Chests, 22 Small Chests, and 22 Skulltulas from dungeons while simultaneously following the these rules:

Big Chests + Small Chests = Skulltulas

OR

Big Chests + Skulltulas = Small Chests

OR

Small Chests + Skulltulas = Big Chests

Example:

Deku Tree Map and Compass = 2

Deku Tree Recovery Heart Chest = 1

Deku Tree B1 Skulltula = 1

2 = 1 + 1 ✔️

*Jabu Big Chests includes beta 39th HP

**Spirit Temple Big Chests includes Silver Gauntlets and Mirror Shield

***Ganon's Trials and Ganon's Tower are combined into the row labelled Ganon's
